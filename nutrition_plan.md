## Targeted keto (TKD) for hard endurance sessions

Use **maltodextrin** before **VO2max** and **threshold** work only (not easy Z2, not long steady rides/runs at low intensity, not as a default on gym-only days).

* **Dose:** **15–25 g** maltodextrin in water **15–30 min before** the hard block starts.
* **Standard week ([schedule.md](schedule.md)):** Tue AM (swim + cycling threshold), Thu AM (swim + cycling VO2max), Mon PM (running VO2max), Fri PM (running threshold) — take TKD **before** that session (for Tue/Thu, before the **11:00** swim + bike block so carbs are available for the cycling quality segment).
* **A/B weeks (weeks 1–6):** TKD only before the **two** quality endurance sessions that week (per schedule — same timing rule).
* **Rationale:** carbs are burned during high glycolytic work; small pre-doses usually do not disrupt ketosis for the rest of the day; can improve interval quality on keto.

## Maltodextrin timing and dose

| When | What | Amount | Notes |
|------|------|--------|--------|
| **Pre-gym** | Maltodextrin only | **15–20 g** in water, **15–30 min** before gym | Optional; use only if energy feels low. Not required on keto for most sessions. |
| **Pre-endurance (TKD)** | Maltodextrin only | **15–25 g** in water, **15–30 min** before VO2max or threshold | Standard approach for scheduled hard endurance (see above). |
| **Intra-gym** | Maltodextrin only | **15–20 g** sipped during the session | Only if session **>~50 min** or energy drops; mix in water bottle. |
| **Intra-endurance (60–90 min hard)** | Maltodextrin only | **30–40 g/h** sipped across the session | Glucose transporter limit ~60 g/h; no need for fructose under ~90 min at this dose range. |
| **Race / competition** | **Maltodextrin : fructose 2 : 1** | **80–100 g/h total** (e.g. **~53 g maltodextrin + ~27 g fructose** per hour) | Two transport pathways (SGLT1 + GLUT5); use bottles/gels on course. |
| **Brick or hard session >90 min** | **Maltodextrin : fructose 2 : 1** | **60–80 g/h total** in a 2:1 mix | Longer than ~90 min at moderate–high intensity. |
| **Saturday long ride (race-prep block)** | **Maltodextrin : fructose 2 : 1** | **60–80 g/h** if deliberately fuelling high | Only when exiting strict keto for a race block; otherwise Z1–2 rides often need only electrolytes + optional low maltodextrin. |

**Maltodextrin-only summary:** pre-gym (optional), TKD pre-hard, intra-gym if long or flat, intra endurance up to ~90 min hard — all **maltodextrin only**.

**Add fructose (always as ~2 : 1 maltodextrin : fructose by carb weight)** when total carb delivery needs to exceed ~60 g/h for **>90 min** — races, long bricks, race-specific long rides.

**Not needed:** fructose for TKD-sized doses, easy Z2 sessions, typical gym days, sessions **<60 min**.

## Long-ride electrolytes (cycling)

For **Saturday long rides** (3–4 h, or any ride **>90 min** in heat):

* Add roughly **500–700 mg sodium per hour** on top of your baseline **daily salt** (NaCl 70% + KCl 30% blend, see [Daily supplements](#daily-supplements)).
* Carry electrolyte mix in bottles; avoid plain water-only bottles on long / hot rides (sweat sodium loss dominates).

## Notes (diet + supplements)

* Zinc : copper ratio target ~10:1 (stack + diet).
* Prefer water without chlorine.
* Iodine (food + Lugol’s): totals are sometimes discussed in **6–50 mg/day** ranges — use clinician guidance.
* **Exogenous ketones (ketone esters):** competitions / priority sessions only.
* **Pantothenic acid (B5):** 400–500 mg if targeting reduction of burning sensation (context-specific).
