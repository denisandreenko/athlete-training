## Supplement timing

* **Pre-gym / pre-endurance:** caffeine + citrulline malate; creatine can be partial here or with a meal.
* **Pre-gym / pre-calisthenics (30–45 min before):** beef collagen + vitamin C; magnesium malate 200 mg.
* **Pre-gym (optional carbs):** 15–20 g maltodextrin in water 15–30 min before gym — only if energy feels low.
* **TKD (hard endurance only):** 15–25 g maltodextrin 15–30 min before VO2max or threshold work.
* **With largest meal or evening:** magnesium glycinate + zinc + copper + B6; omega-3; D3 + K2; beta-alanine if not taken pre-workout.
* **Brazil nuts:** with any meal.

## Targeted keto (TKD) for hard endurance sessions

Use maltodextrin **only before VO2max and threshold** cycling. Not for easy Z2, long steady rides at low intensity, or gym-only days by default.

| When | What | Amount | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| **Pre-gym (optional)** | Maltodextrin | 15–20 g, 15–30 min before | Only if energy feels low |
| **Pre-endurance (TKD)** | Maltodextrin | 15–25 g, 15–30 min before | Standard for VO2max / threshold |
| **Intra-gym** | Maltodextrin | 15–20 g sipped | Only if session >~50 min or energy drops |
| **Intra-endurance (60–90 min hard)** | Maltodextrin | 30–40 g/hr sipped | SGLT1 limit ~60 g/hr; no fructose needed |
| **Race / competition** | Maltodextrin : fructose 2:1 | 80–100 g/hr (e.g. ~53 g malto + ~27 g fructose/hr) | Two transport pathways |
| **Brick or hard session >90 min** | Maltodextrin : fructose 2:1 | 60–80 g/hr | Use when exceeding ~90 min at effort |
| **Saturday long ride (race-prep)** | Maltodextrin : fructose 2:1 | 60–80 g/hr | Only when deliberately fuelling high |

**Rule:** add fructose (as 2:1 malto:fructose by carb weight) only when total carb delivery needs to exceed ~60 g/hr for >90 min. Not needed for TKD-sized doses, easy Z2, or sessions under 60 min.

## Long-ride electrolytes

For Saturday long rides (3–4 h, or any ride >90 min in heat):

* ~500–700 mg sodium/hr on top of baseline daily salt
* Carry electrolyte mix in bottles; avoid plain water-only on long/hot rides

## Body composition

Current: 87 kg, ~11% body fat. Goal: maintain lean, athletic composition while supporting strength, hypertrophy, and cycling volume.

Adjustment rules:
* Weight drops unintentionally → increase calories (add fat first)
* Performance drops → check electrolytes, sleep, carbs around hard sessions, total calories
* Fat gain occurs → reduce added fats first, not protein
* High-volume gravel blocks → do not aggressively cut calories

Protein target: ~1.8–2.4 g/kg/day (~157–209 g). Current intake (~214 g) is slightly above but acceptable if digestion and recovery are good. Zinc:copper ratio target ~10:1. Prefer water without chlorine.
