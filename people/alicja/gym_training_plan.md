# Alicja — Gym Training Plan (Novice Strength / Hypertrophy)

Two full-body sessions (**A** and **B**), on the fixed schedule **Monday (A)** and **Thursday (B)** — three days apart for recovery. Goal: build a strength and muscle base with clean technique. Keep **2–3 reps in reserve** on every set — never train to failure. Log loads/reps in `workout_log.md`.

**Warm-up (every session, ~5 min):** 5 min easy cardio (walk/bike) + leg swings 10/side, arm circles, bodyweight squats × 10, scapular pulls × 10, glute bridge × 10.

## Bodyweight progression ladders

Advance only when you hit the **top of the rep range with clean form and 2+ reps in reserve**.

### Push-up
| Stage | Target |
|---|---|
| Wall push-up | 3 × 12 |
| Incline push-up (hands on bench) | 3 × 10 |
| Knee push-up ← *current* | 3 × 10 |
| Negative full push-up (slow lower) | 3 × 5 |
| Full push-up | 3 × 8 |

### Pull-up
| Stage | Target |
|---|---|
| Inverted row (bar at waist, feet down) | 3 × 10 |
| Band/machine assisted pull-up ← *current* | 3 × 8, reduce assistance over time |
| Negative pull-up (slow lower from top) | 3 × 5 |
| Full pull-up | 1 → 3+ |

### Core
Progress sit-ups and planks by adding reps/time first, then harder variations (dead bug → hollow hold → leg raises).

---

## Progression & deload

- **Double progression:** add reps within the range on all sets, then add a little load (or move to a harder ladder stage for bodyweight moves).
- Increase load in small jumps (1–2 kg on DBs).
- **Deload / easy week** every ~4–6 weeks or when run-down: cut a set per exercise, keep technique.
- If a movement causes joint pain (not muscle effort), regress a stage and check form.
