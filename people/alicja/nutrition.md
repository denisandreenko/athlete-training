# Alicja — Nutrition (Ketogenic)

General keto framework, per-100 g food table, TKD fueling, electrolytes, and body-composition rules are in `shared/keto_nutrition_reference.md`. This file holds Alicja's daily intake and supplement doses.

Diet style: ketogenic, 3 meals/day. Targets are calculated from her stats (164 cm, 52 kg, 24 yo, ~23% body fat), not a flat scale of Denis's plan — she's a novice building a muscle base, so calories sit at a **small surplus** over maintenance to support muscle gain while staying lean.

| Metric | Daily target (approx) |
|--------|-------------|
| Protein | ~112 g (~2.1 g/kg) |
| Carbs | ~22 g net |
| Fat | ~144 g |
| Fiber | ~17 g |
| Calories | ~1950 kcal |

> **Why ~1950, not 70% of Denis:** her estimated maintenance (TDEE) is ~1,800 kcal; 70% of Denis's 3,278 would be a much larger surplus. ~1,950 is a small surplus — ideal for a novice building a muscle base while staying lean — with protein kept high (~2.1 g/kg). **Adjust by the mirror and the scale over 2–3 weeks:** to lean out, drop the Meal 1 butter to ~60 g (≈1,800 kcal); to gain faster, add a little more fat.

## Daily supplements (same stack as Denis; doses scaled to her size)

Confirm with bloodwork before sustained high-dose vitamin D.

* **D3 + K2:** ~4000–5000 IU D3 + 100–200 µg K2
* **Omega 3:** ~1500–2000 mg EPA/DHA
* **Creatine:** 3–5 g
* **Daily salt (low-sodium blend):** NaCl 70% + KCl 30%, iodized — food seasoning and training water
* **Magnesium glycinate stack:** ~200–300 mg magnesium + zinc + copper + B6 (keep zinc:copper ~10:1)
* **Magnesium malate:** ~150 mg (pre-training on harder days)
* **Citrulline malate:** 4–6 g (pre-workout, optional)
* **Beta-alanine:** 3–4 g
* **Caffeine:** ~80–100 mg (lower than Denis; adjust to tolerance)
* **Beef collagen:** ~6,000 mg + vitamin C (pre-training)
* 1–2 Brazil nuts

Timing, food reference table, and electrolyte targets: see `shared/keto_nutrition_reference.md`.

**Fueling note:** as a beginner doing mostly easy running and novice gym work, she rarely needs targeted carbs — easy sessions run fine fat-adapted. Add a small pre-session carb dose only if/when she does genuinely hard intervals and energy feels low.
