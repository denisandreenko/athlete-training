# Alicja — Training Plan

General methodology is in `shared/training_principles.md`. This file holds Alicja's weekly structure. She trains on a **fixed weekly schedule** (below).

## Priorities

1. Strength / muscle base (`gym_training_plan.md`)
2. Mobility & splits (`mobility_splits.md`) — can be daily
3. Running for heart health (`running_plan.md`)
4. Recovery & consistency

## Fixed weekly schedule

| Day | Session | Why it sits here |
|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | Gym A (full body) | Fresh after the weekend → best strength quality |
| **Tue** | Easy run, Zone 2 (4–7 km) + strides | Easy effort aids recovery from Mon's gym; never hard the day after legs |
| **Wed** | Rest + light mobility | Primes fresh legs for Thursday's gym |
| **Thu** | Gym B (full body) | Fresh after Wed rest → protects strength (priority #1) |
| **Fri** | Run — easy in base phase; **quality** (threshold → later VO2max) once progressing | Saturday rest follows; if legs feel heavy from Thu's gym, keep it easy |
| **Sat** | Rest | Full recovery day |
| **Sun** | Full mobility & splits (longer session) | Dedicated flexibility work (priority #2) |

Plus a short **daily mobility minimum** every day (~8–10 min, see `mobility_splits.md`).

**Why this layout (strength is priority #1):**
- Both gym days are preceded by a rest or light day (Mon after the weekend, Thu after Wed rest) so legs are fresh for lifting.
- The two runs sit *after* gym days but each is followed by a rest day, and only the easy run lands right after a gym day in the base phase.
- Gym and hard running never share a day; there are always ≥2 days between the two gym sessions.
- Only mild residual conflict: Friday's quality run follows Thursday's gym — if legs are heavy, keep Friday easy. Strength comes first.

## Readiness (no wearable)

Use the subjective fallback in `shared/training_principles.md`: each morning check sleep, soreness, energy, motivation. If two or more are poor, make the day easy — keep mobility, drop intensity, or swap a gym day for an easy walk.

## Progression focus

- **Strength:** the headline goal — add reps then load on the main movements (see `gym_training_plan.md`).
- **Mobility:** consistency beats intensity; never force a stretch into pain.
- **Running:** extend easy duration before adding speed.

## Do not do by default

- Don't add hard running and hard gym in the same 24 h while still a novice.
- Don't chase soreness or train a movement to failure — technique and consistency first.
- Don't skip mobility on busy weeks; it's a primary goal, shorten it rather than drop it.
