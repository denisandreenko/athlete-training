# Gym Training Plan (Strength / Hypertrophy)

**Split:** Mon **Day A** (squat / horizontal push / vertical pull), Wed **Day B** (pull / overhead press / arms / core), Fri **Day C** (hinge / incline push / single-leg). **~59 working sets/week** across three ~50–55 min sessions (including short activation + mobility). Squat pattern and hinge pattern on **different days** to limit lumbar stress. Heavy leg days (Mon squat, Fri hinge) sit either side of cycling — see *Fatigue-stacking guardrails* in `training_plan.md`.

**Progress:** log loads in `workout_log.md`.

---

## Day A — Monday (18 sets)

1. **Activation (~3 min):** single-leg glute bridge (3 s hold at top) **10/side**; Copenhagen side plank (bent knee) **1 × 20–30 s/side**.
2. **Hack squat:** 3 × 6–8 @ RPE 7–8 (2–3 min rest) — *keep RPE ≤ 8: Tuesday is VO2max. Machine load ≠ barbell squat.*
3. **Bench press:** 3 × 5–6 (2–3 min rest) — *start ~10% below prior flat bench (e.g. ~75 kg if 82.5 was grind).*
4. **Single-leg hip thrust:** 3 × 10–12/leg (90 s) — *weeks 1–2: bodyweight; from week 3 add bar 20–40 kg as tolerated.*
5. **Chin-ups:** 3 × 6–8 (2 min rest) — *bodyweight or light load to start.*
6. **Seated leg curl:** 2 × 12–15 (60 s) — *start ~40–45 kg if prior ~50 kg.*
7. **Cable lateral raises:** 2 × 12–15 (60 s)
8. **Pallof press:** 2 × 10/side (60 s) — *anti-rotation core.*

*Removed Day A standing calf raise (2 sets): soleus is trained Friday (seated calf) and calves get heavy work cycling — low marginal value, and trimming Monday leg volume protects Tuesday VO2max. Re-add if calf hypertrophy becomes a specific goal.*

**Mobility (10 min after session + after Saturday long ride):** half-kneeling hip flexor 60 s/side; pigeon or 90/90 60 s/side; couch stretch 60 s/side; thoracic open book 10/side; cat-cow 10 cycles.

---

## Day B — Wednesday (20 sets)

*No heavy axial loading.*

1. **Weighted pull-ups:** 3 × 5–6 (2–3 min rest) — *start ~+20 kg if prior +25 kg grind.*
2. **Chest-supported row:** 3 × 8–10 (90 s)
3. **Seated DB OHP:** 2 × 8–10 (90 s) — *start ~20 kg DBs; handstand strength base.*
4. **Cable hip abduction:** 2 × 12–15/side (60 s)
5. **Face pulls:** 2 × 12–15 (60 s)
6. **Cable lateral raises:** 2 × 12–15 (60 s)
7. **Incline DB curl:** 2 × 10–12 (60 s)
8. **Overhead tricep extension:** 2 × 10–12 (60 s)
9. **Ab wheel rollout:** 2 × 8–10 (60 s) — *anti-extension core.*

**Mobility:** same 10 min routine as Day A.

---

## Day C — Friday (21 sets)

1. **Activation (~3 min):** same as Day A (glute bridge + Copenhagen side plank).
2. **Trap bar deadlift:** 3 × 4–5 @ RPE 7–8 (3–4 min rest) — *start ~100–110 kg; adjust by RPE. Saturday is the long ride — keep these as quality reps, not grinders, and on amber/red readiness drop to 2 sets.*
3. **Incline DB press:** 3 × 8–10 (90 s) — *ref prior ~28 kg DBs; drop ~10% if needed.*
4. **Bulgarian split squats:** 3 × 8–10/leg (90 s) — *ref 20 kg DBs.*
5. **Nordic hamstring curl (assisted):** 2 × 5–8 (90 s)
6. **Dips:** 2 × 8–12 (90 s)
7. **Leg extension:** 2 × 12–15 (60 s) — *start ~45 kg if prior ~55 kg.*
8. **DB lateral raises:** 2 × 12–15 (60 s)
9. **Seated calf raise:** 2 × 12–15 (60 s)
10. **Cable crunch:** 2 × 15–20 (60 s) — *flexion core.*

**Mobility:** same 10 min routine as Day A.

## Activation and pre-cycling prep

**Before Day A and Day C (gym):** ~3 min total — single-leg glute bridge 10/side (3 s hold at top); Copenhagen side plank (bent knee) 1 × 20–30 s/side.

**Before weekday cycling (Tue, Thu):** hip flexor stretch **30 s/side** + leg swings **10/side** (~2 min).

**Before Saturday long ride:** hip flexor stretch 30 s/side; leg swings front/back + lateral 10/side; deep squat hold **60 s**; single-leg glute bridge **10/side** (~4 min).

---

## Post-recovery reintroductions (week 7–8+, when pain-free)

* **Plyometrics (after activation, before first main lift):** pick one — band-resisted pogos, box jumps, medicine ball slam, plyo push-ups — 2–3 × 3–5 reps (~3–4 min). Prefer Day A and/or Day C.
* **Bilateral barbell hip thrust** on Day A (3 × 10–12, e.g. from ~80 kg) replacing single-leg hip thrust once glute pattern is stable.
* **Back squat (optional):** only if symptom-free; start ~85–90 kg or alternate weeks with hack squat; stop if sacral/coccyx symptoms return.
* **Handstand skill (outside gym):** wall holds, wall walks, pike push-up progressions 10–15 min, 2–3×/week on non-gym days or after mobility.

---

## General guidelines

* No supersets in the written plan — full rest between exercises as prescribed.  
* **Weeks 1–6 (initial block):** Friday after gym = **REST** (no PM endurance).  
* **Compounds (hack squat, bench, trap bar deadlift, pull-ups):** hit the prescribed **RPE** each session; add weight when all sets reach the **top of the rep range** at that RPE.  
* **Accessories:** double progression — add reps within the prescribed range on all sets, then increase load.  
* **Deload:** every **4th week**, reduce compound loads ~**20%** or remove **1 set** per exercise for that week.
